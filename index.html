项目介绍 Project Introduction

紫金国际中心商业裙楼，坐落于三亚市核心商务区，是一座集高端零售、特色餐饮、休闲娱乐于一体的现代化商业综合体。 ZIJIN International Center Commercial Podium, located in the core business district of Sanya, is a modern commercial complex integrating high-end retail, specialty dining, and leisure entertainment.

黄金区位 Prime Location

位于海南省三亚市天涯区迎宾路9号，中央商务区核心位置，毗邻海月广场。 Located at No. 9 Yingbin Road, Tianya District, Sanya City, Hainan Province, in the core of the Central Business District, adjacent to Haiyue Square.

建筑特色 Architectural Features

全玻璃幕墙设计，现代感十足；中庭透视设计提升空间采光；屋顶花园提供绿色休闲空间。 All-glass curtain wall design with a modern feel; atrium perspective design enhances natural lighting; rooftop garden provides green leisure space.

空间优势 Space Advantages

一层、二层层高约5.4米，三层约4.5米，提供全能百变空间；面积区间300-732㎡，满足多元业态需求。 First and second floors with a height of approximately 5.4 meters, third floor about 4.5 meters, providing versatile space; area ranging from 300-732㎡, meeting diverse business needs.

投资优势 Investment Advantages

紫金国际中心商业裙楼凭借其优越的地理位置、独特的设计理念和完善的配套设施，为品牌商家提供了极具投资价值的商业空间。 ZIJIN International Center Commercial Podium, with its superior geographical location, unique design concept, and complete supporting facilities, provides brand merchants with highly valuable commercial space for investment.

高投资回报率 High ROI

三亚作为国际旅游城市，商业价值持续攀升，投资回报率远高于全国平均水平。 As an international tourist city, Sanya's commercial value continues to rise, with investment returns far higher than the national average.

稳定客流保障 Stable Customer Flow

每年2000万+游客量，加上本地高端消费群体，为商业提供稳定的客流支持。 With over 20 million tourists annually, plus local high-end consumer groups, we provide stable customer flow support for businesses.

优质物业品质 Premium Property Quality

现代化建筑设计，高品质装修标准，完善的物业管理，为品牌提供优质经营环境。 Modern architectural design, high-quality decoration standards, and complete property management provide brands with an excellent business environment.

灵活招商政策 Flexible Investment Policies

前三年租金优惠，装修补贴，运营支持等多重优惠政策，助力品牌稳健发展。 Multiple preferential policies such as rent discounts for the first three years, decoration subsidies, and operational support to help brands develop steadily.

统一营销推广 Unified Marketing

专业运营团队，全年营销活动规划，媒体宣传推广，提升品牌知名度和销售额。 Professional operation team, annual marketing activity planning, media promotion to enhance brand awareness and sales.

鼓励创新业态入驻，提供政策支持和资源对接，共同打造差异化商业体验。 Encourage innovative business formats to settle in, provide policy support and resource matching, and jointly create a differentiated commercial experience.
